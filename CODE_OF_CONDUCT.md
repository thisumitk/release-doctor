# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to keep Release Doctor a practical and respectful project for npm maintainers.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Focus feedback on the work and its impact.
- Assume maintainers and contributors are operating in good faith.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Public disclosure of private security reports before a fix is available.
- Spam or intentionally disruptive participation.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block participants who violate this code of conduct.
